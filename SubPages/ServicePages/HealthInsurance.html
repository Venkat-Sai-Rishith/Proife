SHORT TERM MEDICAL INSURANCE

Short-term medical insurance, also known as temporary health insurance, is a type of insurance policy that provides coverage for a limited period of time, typically anywhere from 30 days to 12 months. At Prolife Insurance Agency in Georgia, we offer short-term medical insurance policies that can help bridge the gap in coverage for individuals who are between jobs, waiting for employer-provided coverage to begin, or experiencing other life events that leave them temporarily uninsured.
